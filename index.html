ROE — Risk of Ruin
Probability of a player running out of balance within a limited number of spins.

ROE = % of players who reach zero before the spin limit (bank × 20).

Mean and median are calculated only for ruined players.
Players who survive to the limit are treated as censored — excluded from histogram.

Custom JSON format:
{ "bankrupt": [45, 120, 88, ...], "total": 1000 }
Or just an array of spins-to-ruin (ROE treated as 100%).
